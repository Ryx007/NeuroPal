What changed / mapping for the dev

  • D2 — Tweaks → Settings drawer destination. Same content, now a full screen reached via Drawer → Settings (D1), not the header hamburger.
  • Every control + dispatch preserved (→ `02` Tweaks table): Theme→`setTheme`, Accent→`setAccent`, Reader font→`setReaderFont` (5 faces incl. Atkinson + Dyslexic), Reader layout→`setReaderLayout`, Density→`setDensity`, Font size→`setFontSize`, Line spacing→`setLineSpacing`, WPM→`setWpm`, Voice→`setVoice`. Data from `selectUiState`.
  • Surfaced more prominently (per `02`): theme, reader font, and size also appear in the Reader’s “Display options” (D8) — same store, two entry points.
  • Accessibility: dyslexia font, contrast theme, size/spacing/WPM are the reason these are their own screen; every control keeps its `accessibilityLabel`. D6 applies to any field.
