Emergency Protocol

Red State Regulation

When your nervous system is overwhelmed, we focus on physical biology first. This protocol is designed to cool your system down and anchor your body.

ac_unit

Step 1: Temperature

60 Seconds

Splash cold water on your face or hold an ice pack to your eyes. This triggers the 'Mammalian Dive Reflex' to instantly slow your heart rate.

directions_run

Step 2: Intense Exercise

2 Minutes

Engage in short, high-intensity movement. Burpees, jumping jacks, or running in place. Burn off the excess sympathetic energy.

air

Step 3: Paced Breathing

4 Minutes

Breathe in for 4 seconds, hold for 2, and exhale for 6. Focus on making your exhale longer than your inhale to signal safety to your brain.

accessibility_new

Step 4: Paired Relaxation

3 Minutes

Tense a muscle group as hard as you can for 5 seconds, then release completely. Notice the sensation of the tension leaving your body.

"You are doing a great job managing this moment. This state is temporary and your body is capable of finding its way back to calm."
